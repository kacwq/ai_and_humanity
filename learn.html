AI × Humanity: Alpha or Omega

Shaping Our Destiny in the Age of AI

Are we at the dawn of a new golden age — or the beginning of an ominous end? This book doesn't offer easy answers. It asks the harder question: what kind of future do we actually want, and are we making the choices that lead there?

Written by two humans, assisted by AI — and honest about both. AI × Humanity is itself an experiment in what human-AI collaboration can produce when it's done thoughtfully.

✦ Approximately 90% of the book's content was AI-generated, guided by human direction, expertise, and intent — a demonstration of the very thesis it argues.
